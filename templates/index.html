Welcome to the International Biathlon Union Website!

Biathlon is a Winter Olympic sport that combines cross-country skiing (freestyle technique) with rifle shooting. It requires athletes to alternate between high-intensity skiing and calm, precise marksmanship, typically shooting at 50-meter targets in both prone and standing positions, with penalties for misses.

In this Website can be found 2025/2026 Season result statistics.

More specificaly:
1.The total score for Female players and Male players.
2.The Team Relay standing for Men, Women, And Mixed teams.
3.Nation Cup standing which determin how many sportists each Nation can allow to participate.
